The MSSD Dress Code

In order to prepare our students for professional environments after graduation from high school, the MSSD School Board has created a system-wide dress code. Below is a succinct summary of the rules of the dress code per school, and a downloadable vesrion of the full code can be found here.

MES

  • Clothes must be clean, in good shape, and fit properly.
  • All clothes must be worn as intended (i.e. zippers must be zipped, laces must be tied, etc).
  • Students may wear solid khaki, navy, grey, black, or earth tone pants.
  • Knee-length, solid colored skirts, dress shorts, capri pants, jumpers, and denim jeans are allowed.
  • All pants must be straight legged or flared.
  • All solid colored shirts with a collar are allowed provided that they have sleeves.
  • The top button of buttoned shirts may be worn unbuttoned.
  • Solid colored shirts may be worn as undershirts.
  • Milan-themed tee shirts are allowed.
  • Any solid colored, hoodless covering with or without stripes on the collar or cuffs is allowed over the basic shirt.

MMS & MHS

  • All rules for Milan Middle School and Milan High School are identical to those of Milan Elementary School with the following exceptions:
    • Denim pants/jeans are not allowed.
    • All shirts must be tucked in.
    • Students must wear belts that do not feature any offensive or gang related symbols.

Logos

  • No logos, including Milan related logos, may exceed the size of a standard business card.
  • No monograms, other than an "M" for Milan, are allowed.
  • Logos on shoes and small logos on other clothing items are allowed.

Shoes

  • Heels higher than 1.5 inches are not allowed.
  • No house shoes are allowed.

Jewelry

  • Gang or drug related jewelry is not allowed.
  • No dangerous or distracting accessories may be worn.
  • All piercings are prohibited with the exception of earrings. This includes fake piercings.
  • No grills or gauges are allowed.

Prohibited Items

  • No heavy coats or raincoats may be worn in the building.
  • No form of headwear is permitted.
  • Any gang or drug related items are not allowed.
  • No gloves may be worn inside school buildings.
  • No cap sleeves are allowed on shirts.
  • No cargo pants/shorts, or bell bottoms may be worn.
  • Rolling backpacks are not allowed unless approved by school administration.
  • Unhemmed pants, frayed bottoms, and cutoffs are not allowed.

Special Days

Special days may be scheduled by school administration that will allow specified variation in the dress code.

Exemptions

If your religion/religious beliefs require you to perform certain actions that are contrary to MSSD's dress code, that is no problem! You simply need to file for an exemption. Information on this can be found in the original dress code document.
